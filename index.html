    About Us

    Mandragora ceramics is a passion project of a small group of people who love their craft. Our Golas are:

    • to make beautiful artwork accessible for everyone.
    • to help ceramics lovers learn the craft and develop their creativity.
    • to grow and achieve new heights together.

    Our dream is to get more people involved in pottery making craft as it is a beautiful outlet of creativity, fuel for imagination and an amazing tool for relaxation and contentment.
